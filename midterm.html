Used Book Sales




Come to our monthly used book sale! Choose from over 50,000 used books, records, videos, magazines and more! Items are donated from individuals, estates, companies, and the Palo Alto library, and large book donations are welcome.

Rooms



In our main room (located along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center), we have hardbound books starting at $1 and paperback books starting at 50 cents.

In our new room (located near the Cubberley track, Pavilion, and Greendell School), we have children's books, music, videos, and toys.
Items start at just 50 cents, including:

  • picture books
  • school age fiction
  • school age nonfiction
  • non-English language books
  • CDs
  • DVDs
  • books for parents and teachers

There is a limit of 12 books on Saturday, during the first hour of the sale. This room opens 1 hour earlier than the main room on Saturday.

In rooms K6 and K7, we have bargain books, including hardbound books for $1, paperbacks for 50 cents, and children's books for 25 cents. Prices are half-off on Sunday. Grocery bags can be purchased for $5 each and can be filled with books. These rooms open 1 hour earlier than the main room on Saturday.

Instructions


The following instructions may apply:
  • Some customers come very early on Saturday to ensure a place in line.
  • Each person receives no more than 2 tickets at the Main Room beginning at 8 am on Saturday.
  • The number on the ticket reserves your place in line, allowing you to get coffee or visit our other rooms, but please be sure to arrive before the Main Room opens. If you miss the time when your number is allowed, you forfeit your place in line. If you don't arrive in time to get a ticket, please join the latter part of the line.
  • Because of our severe space limitations and issues of health and safety, we will only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at one time.
  • On Saturdays, customers will be admitted in the order of their numbered tickets from 8 am to 11 am.
  • There is a limit of 12 books at a time.
  • After items are purchased, customers should exit through the east door.
  • Customers may join the end of any remaining line at the north door and reenter in that order.
  • The limit of 12 books at a time will continue past noon if a line remains outside.
  • You can purchase gift certificates at the sale in $5 denominations.

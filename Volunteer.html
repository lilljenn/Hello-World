Volunteer!

Welcome to Hello-World, where you can lend a hand or donate what you can!



Check out the places you can volunteer at and the items you can donate! Be sure to fill out an application to customize your experiences!


More volunteering options can be found on Volunteer Match. More information regarding donating handmade (crocheted/knitted) items/supplies can be found on Craft Industry Alliance, and more options to send handmade cards can be found on Create to Donate. These open external links, and are linked to in the nav bar and footer of this webpage, as well.


Thank you for visiting Hello World!






About

This is a website created for LIS/IM 753 (Internet Fundamentals) at Dominican University. This website aims to present the different options in regards to places to volunteer or donate handmade items.
